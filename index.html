Kele Xu

Brief Bio

Currently, I serve as an Associate Professor at the State Key Laboratory of Complex & Critical Software Environment at NUDT, China.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Université Pierre et Marie Curie, Paris, France, working with Prof. Bruce Denby and Prof. Gerard Dreyfus (IEEE Fellow). My research focuses on Multimodal machine learning, Computer audition, and AI4Science.

In addition to academic pursuits, I actively participate in machine learning competition, having achieved top ranks in data mining and machine learning competitions, including the ACM KDD Cup, Kaggle, Tianchi, and CCF BDCI. I am also a Kaggle Grandmaster.

If you're interested in joining us, please drop me an email at: kele.xu at ieee dot org
