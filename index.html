Brief Bio:

I have received my Ph.D. degree from Université Pierre et Marie CURIE, Paris, France [Link] on Dec. 2016. My current research interests include silent speech interface (SSI), audio signal processing and incremental learning. I am also interested in the applications of machine learning for audio signal processing, time series analyse and medical image processing.

During my part-time, I am a competition-driven researcher. I have won many data mining / machine learning competitions during last years, including ACM KDD Cup [Link], Kaggle [Link], Tianchi [Link] and CCF BDCI (China Computer Federation Computing Intelligence Contest). I am also a Kaggle GrandMaster [Link].

Research Interests:

  • Audio Signal Processing: Audio Classification, Sound Event Detection.
  • Speech Communication: Speech Production, Silent Speech Interface.
  • Multi-modal Learning: Multi-modal Speech, Cross-Modal Representation Learning.
  • Incremental learning: Class incremental Learning, Continuous Reinforcement Learning.
