Brief Bio:

I have received my Ph.D. degree from Université Pierre et Marie CURIE, Paris, France [Link] on Dec. 2016. My current research interests include large-scale distributed machine learning, silent speech interface (SSI) and multi-modal machine learning. I am also interested in the applications of machine learning for audio signal processing, time series analyse and medical image processing.

I have won many data mining / machine learning competitions during last years, including ACM KDD Cup [Link], Kaggle [Link], Tianchi [Link] and CCF BDCI (China Computer Federation Computing Intelligence Contest). I am also a Kaggle GrandMaster [Link].

Research Interests:

  • Speech Signal Processing: Speech Production, Silent speech Interface,.
  • Multi-modal AI: Multi-modal learning, Collaborative learning.
  • Image Processing: Medical image analysis, Remote sensing image analysis.
